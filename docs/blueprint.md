# **App Name**: Mohak

## Core Features:

- Marketplace Redirect Hub: Smart interaction points on every product card allowing direct navigation to Flipkart, Meesho, or launching WhatsApp and Instagram DM chats.
- Interactive Jewelry Catalog: A high-fidelity, Pinterest-style visual gallery featuring Soft Ivory cards with Signature Gold borders and elegant hover-to-reveal details.
- AI Vibe Stylist: An AI-powered fashion assistant tool that uses reasoning to analyze user-provided occasions or outfit descriptions and suggest perfectly matched jewelry pieces.
- Instagram Curated Grid: An aesthetic social wall that dynamically showcases the latest brand imagery from Instagram to build social proof and drive follower growth.
- Direct Inquiry System: Streamlined contact modules and floating WhatsApp CTAs that prioritize personal customer engagement over complex automated checkouts.
- Editorial Brand Storytelling: Rich content sections using parallax and staggered typography to communicate the brand's 'Wear the Vibe' identity and feminine luxury aesthetic.
- Mohak Mode Switcher: A premium theme engine allowing users to toggle the boutique interface between 'Midnight Mohak' and 'Soft Ivory' modes.
- SEO & Social Optimization: Integration of Open Graph tags and optimized metadata to ensure jewelry visuals look premium when shared on social platforms.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary palette: Midnight Mohak (#121212) for backgrounds and navigation; Signature Gold (#C9A14A) for logos, accents, and icons.
- Backgrounds: Soft Ivory (#FAF7F2) for clean cards and airy whitespace to maintain a feminine boutique feel.
- Secondary accents: Muted Copper (#B76E48) for high-priority CTA buttons and Champagne Beige (#E8DCC8) for soft gradients and hover highlights.
- Headlines use 'Playfair Display' for a classic serif luxury feel; body copy uses 'Inter' for a clean, modern, and readable sans-serif experience.
- Ultra-thin, minimalist line icons in gold, inspired by high-end fashion boutiques to represent categories like jewelry types and contact methods.
- A mobile-first, Pinterest-inspired asymmetrical grid utilizing generous negative space, thin gold borders, and floating particles in the hero section.
- Fluid scroll-triggered reveal animations via Framer Motion, combined with soft hover lifts and weightless floating effects for product cards.
